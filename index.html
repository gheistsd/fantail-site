Fantail

Power tools for Xero

A browser extension for bookkeepers and accountants who spend their whole day inside Xero. Jump to any screen with Ctrl+K, switch between client organisations by typing a few letters, pin the screens you use constantly, and keep your hands on the keyboard with shortcuts for save and approve.
