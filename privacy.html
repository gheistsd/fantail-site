Fantail Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

The short version

Fantail does not collect, transmit, store, or sell any data about you or your Xero organisations. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no external scripts.

What the extension stores, and where

Your settings, your pinned favorites, and the names of Xero organisations you have visited are saved in your browser's extension storage (chrome.storage.sync), which your browser may synchronise between your own devices through your own browser account. This data never reaches us. Nobody but you can read it.

What the extension can see

Fantail runs only on go.xero.com pages. It reads the current page address to know which Xero screen you are on, and it reads on-screen elements to perform the actions you trigger (for example, clicking Save when you press Alt+S). None of what it sees leaves your browser.

Network requests

The extension may periodically fetch a small configuration file from our server. This file tells the extension whether any feature should be switched off because a Xero page change broke it. The request contains no personal data, no identifiers, and nothing about your Xero account.

Payments

If a paid tier is offered in future, payment processing will be handled by a dedicated payment provider under its own privacy policy. Fantail itself will still not collect your data.

Changes and contact

If this policy changes, the date above changes with it and the extension's store listing will link to the current version. Questions: fantail-support@gmail.com

Fantail is an independent product and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or supported by Xero Limited.
